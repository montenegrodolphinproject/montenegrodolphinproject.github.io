About

Research beyond boundaries

When it comes to the protection of wildlife, nothing is as essential and effective as a joint effort. Going from the land to the deep sea, the anthropogenic impact on animal survival has dramatically increased and is now palpable as never before. The East Adriatic Sea and its nature make no exception. Transboundary cooperation may represents the only successful tool for the implementation of conservation strategies, particularly for those species whose continuity relies on their ability to migrate and thus, on the absence of boundaries.
